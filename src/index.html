THAT RIGHTS

It is very important to us to support you as a partner optimally in your daily pharmacy routine.

To help us do this, we commissioned a study from the renowned market research institute GfK, which examines the value of Wort & Bild pharmacy customer magazines for your pharmacy.

ADVANTAGES

Better customer loyalty

The readers of the Apotheken Umschau and all other titles of the Wort & Bild publishing house identify themselves with the high-quality content that we offer them. The reading time of the booklet is 85 minutes *. During this time, the interested reader learns a lot about the important achievements of pharmacists, he receives tips on how to stay fit and healthy and which therapeutic measures are "state of the art".

Higher sales

Who knows more, can make concrete decisions. The regular readers of the pharmacies Umschau appreciate the offers and services of the pharmacy and therefore invest more for their health. While a non-reader spends an average of 20.40 EUR per month in the pharmacy, the regular reader buys for 38.20 EUR **.

Editorial quality

At the Apotheken Umschau you can be sure that the contributions correspond to the highest journalistic level and that we strengthen the local pharmacy on every page of our magazine. Our editorial quality, which is secured by almost 100 colleagues, makes us just as unique as our clear separation of editorial content and advertisements. We are your partner in communication and customer loyalty.

Germany's Magazine No. 1 - Quality, Orientation and Credibility
